BBOX Annotation

We have developed a bounding box annotation platform, as illustrated in Figure 2. The left panel displays the input image along with user-provided point prompts. The platform integrates the Segment Anything Model (SAM) as the labeling engine, where green dots denote selected areas and red dots represent unselected areas. The right panel showcases the output, consisting of the automatically generated labeled bounding boxes and their corresponding instance masks produced by SAM.

Figure 2. Screenshot of the bounding box annotation platform. Left: input point prompt. Right: the labeled instance BBOX and mask.

Caption Annotation

We have developed a user-friendly caption annotation platform, as depicted in Figure 3. The left panel displays the image alongside its object bounding boxes, while the right panel contains the corresponding intention descriptions for the objects. To ensure annotation quality, the images were divided into subsets of 100 and assigned to experts, limiting the duration of consecutive tasks. Domain experts are required to refine at least one caption for each image. For the remaining captions, experts classify them as either Positive or Negative. For captions labeled as Negative, experts must specify the corresponding error categories.
